About Me

I'm glad you're here.

I've spent decades at the intersection of AI and what it means to be human in a world of rapid change. My book, The Human Cloud, mapped the shift that was quietly rewriting the rules of work before AI became everyone's main topic of conversation.

Now I'm focused on something I care about deeply: helping real people navigate the AI era with curiosity rather than anxiety. Not as an authority with all the answers, but as a fellow traveler who's been thinking about this for a while and wants to share what I've learned.

AI is a tool. You're the one who decides what to do with it. And people who understand how to get the most out of AI? They build great things.
