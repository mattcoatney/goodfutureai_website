About Me

Hi, I'm glad you're here.

I've spent years at the intersection of AI and what it means to be human in a world of rapid technological change. My book, The Human Cloud, mapped the shift already underway — where AI and new ways of working were quietly rewriting the rules.

Now I'm focused on something more urgent: helping real people navigate the AI era with confidence and curiosity, not anxiety. I'm not here to sell you fear, and I'm not here to promise that AI fixes everything. The truth is more interesting — and more actionable.

AI is a tool. You're the craftsperson. And craftspeople who understand their tools? They build incredible things.
