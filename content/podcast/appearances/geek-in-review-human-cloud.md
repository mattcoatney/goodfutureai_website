Episode 136 of The Geek In Review, a legal technology podcast. We explored how freelancing and project-based work combined with AI and machine learning are disrupting how work gets done — including inside law firms, where legal matters are essentially projects and contract attorneys are already functioning as freelancers. Plus a look at what organizations risk if they don't prepare for this shift.
