Episode 330 of Product Mastery Now (formerly The Everyday Innovator). We talked about how the future of work is being reshaped by technology, the gig economy, and AI — and what that means for product managers and business leaders. The conversation centered on the concept of "Changemakers": people who thrive not by clinging to job titles, but by driving value through an entrepreneurial mindset in a project-based world.
